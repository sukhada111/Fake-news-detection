ABOUT US


Fake news, or hoax news, refers to false information or propaganda published under the guise of being authentic news. Fake news spread through social media has become a serious problem, having the potential to result in mob violence, suicides etc. as a result of misinformation circulated on social media.

This website developed by Ms. Shreya Ughade, Ms. Aiswarya Suresh and Ms. Sukhada Virkar, under the guidance of Prof. Garima Rawat (Faculty, IT Department, KJSCE), aims to work on improving the trustworthiness of information in online social networks and news media by timely identifying fake news.

With the help of Machine Learning, we have tried to build a model to learn the representations of news articles, creators and subjects simultaneously using Natural Language Processing and then predicting the nature of the news using classification algorithms. We used a dataset consisting of about 40000 news articles comprising of fake as well as real news on which a ML model was trained to succesfully classify the news as 'fake' or 'true'. The ML algorithm used was Support Vector Classifier which has enabled the model to predict the authenticity with an accuracy of 99.7%. The ML model was then successfully deployed using Flask in order to identify fake news in real time and hence, curb its spread.
